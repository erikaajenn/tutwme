Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 13, 2026

⚠️ This Privacy Policy is a general template and has not been reviewed by an attorney. Please consult a lawyer before relying on this document, especially regarding children's privacy laws (COPPA).

1. Information We Collect

  • Account info: name, email, phone, password (encrypted), grade level
  • Minor verification: date of birth, parent/guardian email and contact info
  • Tutor info: bio, subjects, hourly rate, teaching philosophy, availability
  • Session data: booking times, subjects, session notes, homework, progress checkpoints, reviews
  • Payment info: we do NOT collect or store credit card or bank account numbers. Payments are made via Venmo, CashApp, or Zelle to TutwMe's accounts directly.

2. How We Use Information

  • Create and manage accounts
  • Match students with tutors and facilitate bookings
  • Verify student and tutor identities and parental consent
  • Process and track payments
  • Send booking confirmations, reminders, and session notifications via email
  • Display tutor profiles, reviews, and progress information to relevant parents and students
  • Maintain platform safety and respond to support requests

3. Children's Privacy

TutwMe serves students under 18 with parent or guardian involvement. For students under 18, we collect a parent/guardian email and require parental approval before the account is activated. Parents can see their child's session history, progress, and notes through the Parent Portal. If you believe your child has provided information without your consent, contact support@tutwme.com.

4. Information Sharing

We do not sell personal information. Information is shared only as needed to operate the platform. Approved reviews are shown publicly on tutor profiles. We may use third-party services (EmailJS for email notifications) which process data on our behalf. We may disclose information if required by law or to protect user safety.

5. Data Storage & Security

Passwords are stored using BCrypt encryption. Data is stored in a managed PostgreSQL database (Supabase). While we take reasonable measures to protect your information, no system is completely secure.

6. Your Choices

You can update your account information by contacting us. Tutors can opt out of the public leaderboard from their dashboard. You may request deletion of your account and data by emailing support@tutwme.com.

7. Changes & Contact

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Continued use of TutwMe after changes are posted constitutes acceptance. Questions: support@tutwme.com
