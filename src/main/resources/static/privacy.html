Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 13, 2026

This Privacy Policy is a general template and has not been reviewed by an attorney. Please consult a lawyer before relying on this document for legal protection, especially regarding children's privacy laws (COPPA).

1. Information We Collect

When you use TutwMe, we may collect:

  • Account information: name, email address, phone number, password (encrypted), grade level
  • Verification information: date of birth, school email domain, parent/guardian email and contact info for students under 18
  • Tutor information: bio, subjects, hourly rate, teaching philosophy, availability
  • Booking & session data: session times, subjects, session notes, homework, progress checkpoints, reviews
  • Payment information: we do not collect or store credit card or bank account numbers. Payments are made via Venmo, CashApp, or Zelle directly to TutwMe's accounts, and we retain records of payment status and amounts.

2. How We Use Information

We use collected information to:

  • Create and manage accounts
  • Match students with tutors and facilitate bookings
  • Verify student and tutor identities and parental consent
  • Process and track payments between students/parents, TutwMe, and tutors
  • Send booking confirmations, reminders, and session check-in notifications via email
  • Display tutor profiles, reviews, and progress information to relevant parents and students
  • Maintain platform safety and respond to support requests

3. Children's Privacy

TutwMe is designed to be used by students under 18 with parent or guardian involvement. For students under 18, we collect a parent/guardian email and require parental approval before the account is activated. Parents can access their child's session history, progress, and notes through the Parent Dashboard. If you are a parent and believe your child has provided information without your consent, please contact us at support@tutwme.com so we can address it.

4. Information Sharing

We do not sell personal information. Information is shared only as needed to operate the platform:

  • Tutors and students/parents can see relevant booking, session, and contact details for sessions they're involved in
  • Approved reviews are shown publicly on tutor profiles
  • We may use third-party services (such as EmailJS for email notifications and Cloudinary for file storage) which process data on our behalf
  • We may disclose information if required by law or to protect the safety of our users

5. Data Storage & Security

Account passwords are stored using industry-standard encryption (BCrypt). Data is stored in a PostgreSQL database. While we take reasonable measures to protect your information, no system is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

6. Your Choices

You can update your account information at any time by contacting us. Tutors can opt out of the public leaderboard from their dashboard. You may request deletion of your account and associated data by emailing support@tutwme.com.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Continued use of TutwMe after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.

8. Contact

Questions about this Privacy Policy can be sent to support@tutwme.com.
