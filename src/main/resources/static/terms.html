1. About TutwMe

TutwMe is a local tutoring marketplace operated by Erika Jentugas, connecting students and parents in the Elk Grove and Sacramento area with independent tutors. TutwMe facilitates connections and bookings — we are not an employer of tutors and do not directly provide tutoring services.

2. Eligibility & Accounts

You must provide accurate information when creating an account. Students under 18 require verified parent or guardian consent before booking sessions. Tutors must be approved by TutwMe before their profiles become publicly visible. TutwMe reserves the right to suspend or terminate any account that violates these Terms.

3. Bookings & Payments

Tutors set their own hourly rates. Payment is made directly to TutwMe via Venmo, CashApp, or Zelle before a session is confirmed. TutwMe deducts a 10% platform fee and forwards the remaining 90% to the tutor. Payments are processed manually — TutwMe does not store credit card or bank account information. Cancellations and refunds are handled case-by-case. Contact support@tutwme.com for payment disputes.

4. Tutor Independence

Tutors on TutwMe are independent contractors, not employees or agents of TutwMe. TutwMe does not guarantee the quality, accuracy, or outcomes of any tutoring session. While TutwMe reviews tutor applications, we do not perform formal background checks. Parents and students are encouraged to communicate directly with tutors and use good judgment, especially for in-person sessions.

5. Conduct & Safety

Users agree to treat each other with respect and professionalism. Harassment, discrimination, or unsafe behavior will result in account suspension. For sessions involving minors, we strongly encourage virtual sessions or sessions held in public/supervised locations.

6. Reviews

Reviews submitted by students and tutors are subject to admin approval before publication. TutwMe reserves the right to remove reviews that are abusive, false, or violate these Terms.
