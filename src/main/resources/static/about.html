Our story

Built by our community,
for our community

TutwMe started with a simple idea: Elk Grove and Sacramento families shouldn't have to search outside their own neighborhoods to find tutors they can trust.

👋
Why I started TutwMe

I grew up in this community, and I've seen how hard it can be for families to find tutors who actually understand local schools, local students, and the specific challenges kids in Elk Grove and Sacramento face. Big national tutoring platforms feel impersonal and expensive — and don't connect you with anyone nearby.

TutwMe is my attempt to fix that: a simple, safe, local marketplace where students find tutors from their own neighborhoods, parents have full visibility into their child's progress, and tutors — many of them local college students — can build a real tutoring business close to home.

Our mission

Make high-quality, personalized tutoring accessible and affordable for every student in Elk Grove and Sacramento — while giving local tutors a platform to grow their own tutoring business.

Safety & verification

Because TutwMe serves students of all ages — including minors — safety is built into every part of the platform.

🎓
School email verification — students can verify using their school email (EGUSD, SCUSD, San Juan, and more)
👨‍👩‍👧
Parental consent — students under 18 require parent/guardian approval before booking sessions
🪪
Tutor identity review — every tutor application is manually reviewed and approved by our team before going live
📋
Transparent session notes — parents can see what was covered, homework assigned, and progress made every session
⭐
Moderated reviews — all reviews are approved by our team before being published

Frequently asked questions

Is TutwMe only for Elk Grove and Sacramento?
+
Right now, yes — we're focused on building a strong local community in Elk Grove and Sacramento before expanding to other areas.
How much does TutwMe cost?
+
Tutors set their own hourly rates, typically $40-$70/hr. TutwMe adds a small 10% platform fee to cover payment processing and platform maintenance.
How are tutors vetted?
+
Every tutor submits an application with their background, subjects, and teaching philosophy. Our team manually reviews and approves each profile before it goes live.
Can I switch tutors if it's not a good fit?
+
Of course! You can browse and book with any tutor at any time — there's no long-term commitment required.
I want to become a tutor — how do I apply?
+
Click "Become a Tutor" from the homepage, fill out the application with your subjects, rate, and teaching style. Our team reviews applications within 24-48 hours.
