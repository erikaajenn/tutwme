Our story

Built by a tutor,
for tutors & families.

TutwMe is Elk Grove & Sacramento's hyperlocal tutoring platform — designed to make tutoring personal, transparent, and relationship-first.

✏️ Built by a tutor, for tutors.

👋
Why I built this

I grew up in this community. I tutored here. And I watched families struggle to find tutors they could actually trust — while good local tutors had no real tools to build a sustainable business.

Big national platforms feel cold and impersonal. They don't know Elk Grove. They don't know Sacramento. They don't connect you with someone from your neighborhood who cares about your child's success.

TutwMe is my answer — a platform built on trust, transparency, and long-term relationships. Not transactions.

Our mission

Make great tutoring accessible
to every family in our community.

While giving local tutors the tools to build a real tutoring business — not just a side gig.

What makes TutwMe different

7 principles we're built on

🏘️

Hyperlocal community

We serve Elk Grove, Sacramento, Roseville, Folsom, Davis, and surrounding communities — not the whole country.

💼

Tutor business platform

Scheduling, payments, student CRM, session notes, invoicing, analytics — everything tutors need in one place.

📊

Parent progress portal

Parents see session notes, homework, goals, and long-term progress — not just a booking receipt.

🤝

Relationship-first

Favorite tutors, recurring sessions, long-term progress tracking — we build lasting learning relationships, not one-time bookings.

🎨

Tutor portfolios

Rich profiles with intro videos, teaching prompts, certifications, and success stories — not just a headshot and subject list.

✨

AI tutor matching

Describe your child's needs and personality. AI matches based on teaching style, experience, and fit — not just subject and price.

🎓

Learning community Coming soon

SAT workshops, study groups, parent webinars, local events — TutwMe as a year-round education hub for our community.

FAQ

Common questions

Is TutwMe only for Elk Grove and Sacramento?
+
We currently serve Elk Grove, Sacramento, Roseville, Folsom, Davis, and surrounding areas. We're growing — more cities coming soon.
How much does it cost?
+
Tutors set their own hourly rates (typically $40-$80/hr). TutwMe adds a 10% platform fee to cover operations. Creating an account is completely free.
How are tutors vetted?
+
Every tutor submits a full application — bio, subjects, teaching philosophy, experience, and keywords. Our team manually reviews every profile before it goes live. No tutor appears publicly without approval.
Can I switch tutors?
+
Absolutely — no long-term commitment required. Browse and book with any tutor at any time. We encourage you to find the right fit.
How do I apply as a tutor?
+
Click "Become a Tutor" from the homepage or navigation. Fill out the application with your subjects, rate, bio, and teaching style. Our team reviews within 24-48 hours and reaches out by email.

Ready to join us?

Students, parents, and tutors — there's a place for all of you here.
